Project Details

The College Website Development Project is a comprehensive effort to design and build an informative, user-friendly, and visually appealing website for an educational institution. This project leverages HTML and CSS to create an online platform that provides information about the college, its programs, admissions, faculty, and other essential details.

The website includes various informative pages, such as the homepage, about us, programs, admissions, faculty, and contact information. A clear and user-friendly navigation menu helps visitors find their way around the website easily.

A section highlighting upcoming events, news, and updates related to the college. Galleries with images of the campus, classrooms, extracurricular activities, and more.

The College Website Development Project serves as a vital online presence for the educational institution, providing prospective students, current students, and the general public with essential information about the college's offerings and activities. It reflects the institution's identity and commitment to education.
